隐私政策Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026年5月26日 | 版本 v1.0Last Updated: May 26, 2026 | Version 1.0

MoHan RWA 平台（以下简称“MoHan”、“我们”）尊重并保护您的隐私。本政策解释我们如何收集、使用、存储和披露您的个人信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 身份验证信息：姓名、地址、出生日期、政府签发的身份证件、纳税人识别号（TIN）或 SSN 后四位。
  • 联系信息：电子邮箱、手机号码、邮寄地址。
  • 财务信息：银行账户信息、资产证明、收入来源、年收入及净资产范围（用于合格投资者认证）。
  • 技术信息：IP 地址、设备标识符、浏览器类型、操作系统、访问时间戳及日志记录。
  • 区块链信息：您用于接收代币的钱包地址（链上公开，但我们不将地址与您的身份公开关联）。
  • 尽职调查信息：职业、政治敏感人物（PEP）状态、资金来源说明等，以满足反洗钱（AML）要求。

2. 信息的使用

我们使用您的信息用于：完成 KYC/AML 验证和合格投资者认证；执行代币认购、收益分配及协议转让；发送资产运营报告、重大事项通知和投票提案；检测和防范欺诈、洗钱、制裁违规；改进平台安全性和用户体验。

3. 信息的共享

我们不会出售您的个人信息。仅在以下情形共享：与为我们提供身份验证、反洗钱筛查、资产托管、审计、法律服务的合作方共享（签署保密协议）；应法律、监管或法院要求而披露；在代币协议转让时向受让方披露必要的投资者资质信息；获得您明确同意的情况下。

4. 数据安全与存储

我们采用 AES-256 加密存储个人信息，传输使用 TLS 1.3 协议。数据存储于美国 SOC 2 Type II 认证的数据中心。您需自行保管钱包私钥，平台无法访问您的私钥。

5. 数据保留

在您持有代币期间及账户关闭后至少 7 年（为遵守证券法和反洗钱记录保存要求）。到期后安全删除或匿名化。

6. 您的权利

您有权访问、更正、删除您的个人信息，限制或反对某些处理，以及获取数据便携副本。请通过 yangmohan945@163.com 联系我们，我们将在 30 天内响应。

🔐 本平台已投保数字资产综合险，并通过 SOC 2 Type II 审计。您的数据安全是我们的首要关切。

MoHan RWA Platform respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity: Name, address, date of birth, government-issued ID, taxpayer identification number (or last 4 SSN).
  • Contact: Email, phone number, mailing address.
  • Financial: Bank account details, asset statements, income source, annual income and net worth range (for accredited investor verification).
  • Technical: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, OS, timestamps, log records.
  • Blockchain: Wallet address used to receive tokens (on-chain public, but we do not publicly link it to your identity).
  • Due Diligence: Occupation, PEP status, source of funds description to comply with AML regulations.

2. Use of Information

We use your information for: KYC/AML and accredited investor verification; token subscription, distribution of yields, and private transfers; sending operating reports, material event notices, and voting proposals; detecting fraud, money laundering, and sanctions violations; improving platform security and user experience.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Sharing occurs only with: service providers under confidentiality agreements (identity verification, AML screening, custody, audit, legal); as required by law, regulation, or court order; to disclose necessary investor qualifications to a transferee in a private token transfer; with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security & Storage

We encrypt personal information using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Data is stored in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers in the United States. You are responsible for safekeeping your wallet private keys; we cannot access them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information while you hold tokens and for at least 7 years after account closure (to comply with securities laws and AML recordkeeping). After retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete your data, restrict or object to certain processing, and receive a portable copy. Contact us at yangmohan945@163.com; we will respond within 30 days.
